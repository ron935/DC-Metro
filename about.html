Our Story

Building Excellence Since 1999

DC Metro Construction was founded with a simple mission: to deliver exceptional construction services that exceed client expectations while maintaining the highest standards of quality, safety, and integrity.

What started as a small team of dedicated professionals has grown into one of the most trusted construction firms in the Washington DC metropolitan area. Over the past 25 years, we have completed more than 500 projects ranging from luxury residential homes to major commercial developments.

Our success is built on strong relationships with our clients, subcontractors, and community. We believe that open communication, transparent processes, and a commitment to excellence are the foundations of every successful project.

Our Mission

Creating Lasting Value Through Quality Construction

Our mission is to be the most trusted and respected construction company in the DC metropolitan area by consistently delivering projects that are completed on time, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Our Journey

Company Milestones

A look back at the key moments that shaped DC Metro Construction.

1999

Company Founded

DC Metro Construction was established in Washington, DC with a team of five dedicated professionals.

2005

First Major Commercial Project

Completed our first large-scale commercial development, a 150,000 sq ft office building in Tysons Corner.

2010

LEED Certification

Became one of the first construction firms in the region to achieve LEED certification for sustainable building practices.

2015

Regional Expansion

Expanded operations throughout the entire DC metropolitan area, including Northern Virginia and Maryland.

2020

500th Project Milestone

Celebrated the completion of our 500th project, a state-of-the-art medical facility in Arlington.

2024

25 Years of Excellence

Marking 25 years of building excellence in the DC metropolitan area with 150+ team members.
