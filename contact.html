FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with a project?

Simply fill out our contact form or give us a call. We'll schedule a free consultation to discuss your project requirements, timeline, and budget. From there, we'll provide a detailed proposal and estimate.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes, we provide free initial consultations and project estimates for all potential clients. Our detailed estimates include a comprehensive breakdown of all costs, timelines, and project phases.

Are you licensed and insured?

Yes, DC Metro Construction is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain all required state and local licenses and carry comprehensive liability insurance and workers' compensation coverage.

What is your warranty policy?

We stand behind our work with a comprehensive warranty on all workmanship. Specific warranty terms vary by project type but typically range from 1-5 years. We also honor all manufacturer warranties on materials and equipment.

Can you work within my budget?

We work with clients across a range of budgets. During our initial consultation, we'll discuss your budget and provide value engineering suggestions to help you achieve your goals while staying within your financial parameters.
